Comparison of Different Internet Carriers

     
Internet Carrier Type Speed Price Advantages Disadvantages
Dial-up Internet Slow Cheap Inexpensive and covers a huge area due to existing infrastructure May be slow, especially if using it for streaming movies, videos and big data
Multilink Dial-up Internet Slow - Mid Cheap - Mid Faster than (single) Dial-up Internet Needs multiple modems
Cable Internet Access Mid - Fast Affordable (has multiple packages depending on your needs) Widely Accessible and great customer service from a reputable company due to having many customers Shared bandwidth leads to slower speeds during peak times
Digital Subscriber Line Mid Cheap - Mid Widely Accessible Lower speeds and limited data caps
Fibre to the Home Fast Mid - Expensive Enhanced Security, Reliable, Can handle peak periods Expensive, Complex to install
Satellite broadband Fast Expensive (and has a one-time equipment//installation fee sometimes) Widely available even in remote areas Weather sensitivity and Data caps, Expensive
Hybrid Access Networks Fast Mid - Expensive Works well due to integration of multiple network technologies Using multiple network technologies may be reflected in the price
Wireless ISP Varies Varies Accessible Weather sensitive, May be impeded by line obstruction, Public WISP may not be secure
